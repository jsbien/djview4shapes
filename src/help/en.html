djview4shapes

Janusz S. Bień

This is a fork of djview4 (http://djvu.sourceforge.net/djview4.html) allowing to view the content of the common shapes dictionaries contained in DjVu documents. The dictionaries are created for compression purposes, but can serve also to identify the inventory of character shapes and to help training OCR programs. On MS Windows it may be necessary to set Settings --- Configure --- Path to djview4.

The use of the program is self-explanatory with some minor exceptions (some of them are inherited from djview4):

  • a single left click in the shapes panel toggles between selecting a single shape and a tree of similar shapes with the pointed shape as a root; occurences of the shape are displayed in the concordance panel on the left. graphical, textual and metadata panel,
  • a single right click in the shapes panel exports the selected shapes as graphic files,
  • a single left click in the main panel selects the appropriate shape in the shape panel,
  • a double left click in the concordance panel displays the appropriate page in the main window,
  • a middle click in the side panel text area starts a new instance of djview4 with the appropriate page displayed,
  • pressing the left button allows to pan the text in the window,
  • pressing Ctrl key allows to use the mouse wheel for zooming the current window.

The shapes are displayed for the number of pages specified in the settings. Specifying large numbers can cause long computations.

The recommended way to report bugs and problems is to send them to the mailing list: http://lists.mimuw.edu.pl/listinfo/nmpt-l.
